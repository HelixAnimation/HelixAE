# Organise

## Overview

HelixAE can automatically organise items in your AE Project panel into a clean folder structure. This is useful when footage has been imported manually or things have gotten messy.

---

## How to Organise

1. In the Footage Tracker, right-click any **folder** (3D Renders, 2D Renders, Resources, Comps, or External)
2. Select **Organise...**
3. A **preview dialog** will appear showing the before/after folder structure
4. Click **Confirm** to apply, or **Cancel** to abort

You can also right-click multiple folders at once and choose **Organise All [N] Folders...** to organise them in one step.

---

## Auto-Categorize Resources

For the **Resources** folder specifically, there's an additional option:

- Right-click the Resources folder → **Auto-Categorize**

This automatically sorts loose resource items into subfolders based on their type (images, video, audio, etc.).

---

## Validate Naming Convention

For **3D Renders** and **2D Renders** folders, you can right-click and choose **Validate Naming Convention** to check that all items follow the expected naming structure. Any inconsistencies will be reported.
